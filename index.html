Acoustics

Acoustics is the science of sound—how it’s generated, travels, and interacts with rooms and materials. In audio product design it covers frequency response, distortion, directivity, and psychoacoustics (how we perceive space and tone). The goal is accurate, engaging playback with minimal coloration.
